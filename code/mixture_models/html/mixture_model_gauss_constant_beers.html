Model definition¶

The following model is assumed:

\begin{equation} \large A_{m} \sim \text{U} (0, max(Y)) \\ \large \mu_{m} \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_{m}, 50) \\ \large \sigma_{m} \sim |\mathcal{N}(0, 100)| \\ \large \epsilon \sim |\mathcal{N}(0, 1)| \\ \large \theta = \sum\limits_{m=1}^M [A_m \cdot e^{-\frac{(x_n-\mu_m)^2}{2\cdot\sigma_m^2}}] + a_0 + a_1 \cdot x_n \\ \large y \sim \mathcal{N}(\theta, \epsilon) \end{equation}
